## Care Instructions / Kapde Ki Dekhbhale

To make your TADKEE tunic last longer, follow these care instructions:

*   Machine wash in cold water with a mild detergent.
*   Wash separately from other fabrics and colors.
*   Do not bleach.
*   Tumble dry on low or hang to dry. Don't dry in direct sunlight, warna color fade ho sakta hai.
*   Iron on low heat if needed.

Linen should be washed at temperatures below 40 degrees Celsius. Use natural detergents for delicate fabrics and avoid using softeners. To avoid shrinkage, pre-wash in cold water.
