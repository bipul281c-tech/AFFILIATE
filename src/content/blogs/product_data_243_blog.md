## Care Instructions / Kapde Ki Dekhbhale

Cotton kurtas require a little bit of extra care to make them last longer.

*   **Washing:** Hand wash in cold water with a mild detergent. If using a washing machine, use the delicate cycle. Turn your kurta inside-out to protect the printed surface
*   **Drying:** Don't dry in direct sunlight, as it can fade the color. Dry in shade.
*   **Ironing:** Iron on low heat.
*   **Storage:** Store wrapped in muslin cloths to prevent moisture and color transfer.
