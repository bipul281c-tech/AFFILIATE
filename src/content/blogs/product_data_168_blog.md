## Care Instructions / Kapde Ki Dekhbhale

Cotton kurtas need a little bit of extra care to last longer:

*   **Washing**: Hand wash is always the best option. If using a machine, use a gentle cycle with cold water.
*   **Detergent**: Use a mild detergent. Avoid harsh chemicals or bleach.
*   **Drying**: *Don't dry in direct sunlight*. Dry in the shade to prevent fading.
*   **Ironing**: Iron on low heat. It's best to iron the kurta inside out.
