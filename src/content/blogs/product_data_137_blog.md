## Care Instructions / Kapde Ki Dekhbhale

To make this kurta set last longer, follow these care instructions:

*   **Washing:** Wash it in cold water on a gentle cycle. Hot water can cause the color to fade and the fabric to shrink.
*   **Drying:** Avoid drying it in direct sunlight, as this can also cause the color to fade. Tumble dry on low heat or, *sabse achha*, air dry it.
*   **Ironing:** Iron on a low to medium heat setting.
*   **Storage:** Store it in a cool, dry place.
