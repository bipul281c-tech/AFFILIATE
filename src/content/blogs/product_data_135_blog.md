## Care Instructions / Kapde Ki Dekhbhale

Cotton blend hai toh zyada nakhre nahi karega, but here are some tips to make it last longer:

*   **Washing:** Machine wash in cold water on a gentle cycle. Avoid using bleach.
*   **Drying:** Tumble dry on low heat or, even better, air dry. Don't dry it in direct sunlight, as it can fade the colors.
*   **Ironing:** Iron on low heat. If the fabric is slightly damp, it will be easier to iron out the wrinkles.
