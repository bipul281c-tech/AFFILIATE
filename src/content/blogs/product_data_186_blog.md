## Care Instructions / Kapde Ki Dekhbhale

To make your kurti last longer, follow these care instructions:

*   Machine wash in cold water with similar colors.
*   Use a mild detergent.
*   Do not bleach.
*   Tumble dry on low or hang to dry.
*   Iron on medium heat.
*   Avoid drying in direct sunlight, as it can fade the colors.
