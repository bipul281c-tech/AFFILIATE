## Care Instructions / Kapde Ki Dekhbhale

Rayon is a delicate fabric, so it needs some extra care. Here are some tips to make your BIBA kurta last longer:

*   Hand wash is preferred. If using a washing machine, use a delicate cycle with cold water.
*   Avoid using harsh detergents or bleach.
*   Do not tumble dry. Air dry in the shade to prevent fading.
*   Iron on low heat, inside out.
*   Don't dry in direct sunlight.
