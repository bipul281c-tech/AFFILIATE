## Care Instructions / *Kapde Ki Dekhbhale*

Rayon is a delicate fabric, so it needs some extra care.

*   Hand wash is preferred. If you machine wash, use a delicate cycle with cold water.
*   Don’t use harsh detergents or bleach.
*   Avoid wringing the kurta. Gently squeeze out the water.
*   Don’t dry in direct sunlight. Air dry in the shade to prevent fading.
*   Iron on low heat, inside out.
