## Care Instructions / Kapde Ki Dekhbhale

Rayon is a delicate fabric, so you need to take care of it properly to make it last longer.

*   **Washing:** Hand wash in cold water is best. If you use a machine, use a gentle cycle and put the kurti in a mesh washing bag.
*   **Drying:** Don't wring it out! Lay it flat to dry or hang it in the shade. Don't dry in direct sunlight, as this can fade the color.
*   **Ironing:** Iron on low heat. It's best to iron it inside out or use a pressing cloth to protect the fabric.
