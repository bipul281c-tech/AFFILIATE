## Care Instructions / *Kapde Ki Dekhbhale*

Rayon fabric requires a little extra care to maintain its quality. Here are some tips:

*   Hand wash is preferred, but you can also machine wash on a gentle cycle.
*   Use cold water and a mild detergent.
*   Do not bleach.
*   Do not tumble dry. Hang it to dry in a shaded area. Don't dry in direct sunlight, as it can fade the color.
*   Iron on low heat, inside out.
