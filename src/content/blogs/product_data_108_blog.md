## Care Instructions / Kapde Ki Dekhbhale

To make this kurta set last longer, here are some care instructions:

*   **Washing:** Hand wash is always the best option for delicate fabrics like cotton. If you're using a machine, use a gentle cycle with cold water. Turn the kurta inside out to protect the print.
*   **Detergent:** Use a mild detergent. Avoid harsh chemicals or bleach.
*   **Drying:** Don't dry it in direct sunlight, as this can fade the colors. Dry it in the shade or indoors.
*   **Ironing:** Iron on low heat, inside out.
