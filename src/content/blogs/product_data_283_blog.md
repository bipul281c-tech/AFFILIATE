## Care Instructions / Kapde Ki Dekhbhale

Cotton kurtas need a little bit of extra care to last longer. Here are some tips:

*   **Hand wash** in cold water with a mild detergent.
*   If machine washing, use a gentle cycle and a laundry bag.
*   **Don't dry in direct sunlight** to prevent fading.
*   Iron on low heat, inside out.
*   Store in a cool, dry place.
