## Care Instructions / Kapde Ki Dekhbhale

Linen ko properly care karna zaroori hai to make it last longer. Here are some tips:

*   **Washing:** Hand wash is ideal, but you can also machine wash on a gentle cycle with cold water.
*   **Detergent:** Use a mild detergent. Avoid bleach.
*   **Drying:** Air dry is best. Don't dry in direct sunlight, as it can fade the color.
*   **Ironing:** Iron while the fabric is still slightly damp. Use a steam setting if available.
