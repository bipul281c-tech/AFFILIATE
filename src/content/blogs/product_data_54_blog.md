## Care Instructions / Kapde Ki Dekhbhale

To make this kurta last longer, it's important to take good care of it.

*   **Washing:** Wash in cold water on a gentle cycle. Avoid using harsh detergents or bleach.
*   **Drying:** Air dry is best to prevent shrinkage. If you must use a dryer, tumble dry on low heat. Don't dry in direct sunlight, as it can fade the color.
*   **Ironing:** Iron on low to medium heat. Use a pressing cloth to protect the embroidery.
